🔗 Google Sheets Connection

1 Create your Google Sheet with this column structure:
Row 1 (headers): Month Clinic CCM_Rev RPM_Rev RTM_Rev NH_Rev Dialysis_Rev Payroll Overhead CCM_Pts RPM_Pts NH_Pts Dialysis_Pts Problem Goal
2 Publish to web as CSV
In Google Sheets: File → Share → Publish to web
Choose your sheet tab → select Comma-separated values (.csv) → click Publish
Copy the URL and paste it above.
3 Optional: Google Form for billing team
Create a Google Form linked to your sheet. Go to your Sheet → Insert → Form. Add fields matching the columns above. Share the form link with your billing team — responses will auto-populate the sheet, which the dashboard reads automatically on refresh.
4 Auto-refresh (Apps Script)
In Google Sheets: Extensions → Apps Script. This enables automations. You can also set the dashboard to auto-refresh — change the interval below.
